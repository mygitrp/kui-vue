<cn>
#### 图标
`showIcon` 来设置是否显示图标
</cn>

```tpl
<template>
  <div>
    <Alert type="success" showIcon>Success Text</Alert> 
    <Alert type="info" showIcon>Info Text</Alert>
    <Alert type="warning" showIcon>Warning Text</Alert>
    <Alert type="error" showIcon>Error Text</Alert> 
  </div>
</template>
```
