<cn>
#### 设置图标
通过 `icon` 设置图标
</cn>

```tpl
<template>
  <div>
    <Breadcrumb>
      <BreadcrumbItem to="/" icon="ios-home">Home</BreadcrumbItem>
      <BreadcrumbItem to="/breadcrumb" icon="logo-buffer">breadcrumb</BreadcrumbItem>
      <BreadcrumbItem icon="ios-heart">other</BreadcrumbItem>
    </Breadcrumb>
  </div>
</template>
```
